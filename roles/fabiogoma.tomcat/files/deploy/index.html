Fred Jones

Fred leads the group in solving mysteries and often orders the gang to split up to search for clues. Fred usually takes Daphne and Velma with him while Shaggy and Scooby go off by themselves.

Daphne Blake

Daphne was portrayed as the enthusiastic and very beautiful, but clumsy and danger-prone.

Velma Dinkley

Velma is usually portrayed as a highly intelligent and slightly tomboyish yet beautiful young woman with various interests.
